Advanced ECG & ECHO Diagnostic Services at Pralaksha Multispeciality Hospital

At Pralaksha Multispeciality Hospital, we offer accurate and timely cardiac diagnostics with our state-of-the-art ECG and ECHO services. These essential tools help in early detection and monitoring of heart conditions, allowing for effective treatment planning and improved patient outcomes.

Our experienced cardiology team uses advanced, non-invasive techniques to evaluate heart health, ensuring patient comfort and diagnostic precision at every step.

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

An ECG records the electrical activity of the heart and is used to detect:

  • Irregular heart rhythms (arrhythmias)

  • Heart attacks (current or past)

  • Abnormal heart structure or size

  • Effects of medications on heart rhythm

  • Signs of reduced blood flow to the heart

Echocardiography (ECHO)

ECHO uses ultrasound waves to create real-time images of the heart. It helps assess:

  • Heart function and pumping efficiency

  • Valve function and structural abnormalities

  • Congenital heart defects

  • Fluid around the heart (pericardial effusion)

  • Wall motion abnormalities due to coronary artery disease

Why Choose Pralaksha for ECG & ECHO?

  • Modern ECG and ultrasound machines for accurate readings

  • Quick turnaround times with expert interpretation

  • Experienced cardiology and radiology team

  • Comfortable and safe environment for all age groups

If you're experiencing symptoms like chest pain, palpitations, or breathlessness, don't delay. Schedule your ECG or ECHO at Pralaksha Multispeciality Hospital today and get a clearer picture of your heart health.
