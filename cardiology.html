Comprehensive Cardiology Care at Pralaksha Multispeciality Hospital

At Pralaksha Multispeciality Hospital, we are committed to providing world-class care for all your heart-related concerns. Whether you're dealing with hypertension, coronary artery disease, heart failure, or arrhythmias, our team of experienced cardiologists is dedicated to ensuring your heart health through cutting-edge technology and personalized treatment plans.

We offer a comprehensive range of services for both preventive and interventional cardiology, ensuring that your heart is in the best hands.

Comprehensive Cardiac Services

Our cardiology services are designed to address a wide range of heart conditions. We offer:

  • **Preventive Cardiology** – Regular check-ups, screenings, and lifestyle modifications aimed at preventing heart disease.

  • **Coronary Artery Disease (CAD) Management** – Diagnosis and treatment options such as stent placements and coronary artery bypass surgery (CABG).

  • **Heart Failure Treatment** – Managing heart failure through medication, lifestyle changes, and advanced therapies like heart transplantation or ventricular assist devices (VADs).

  • **Arrhythmia Treatment** – Management of heart rhythm disorders, including atrial fibrillation (AF), ventricular arrhythmias, and other abnormal heart rhythms.

  • **Valvular Heart Disease** – Diagnosis and management of heart valve conditions such as aortic stenosis and mitral valve prolapse.

Advanced Diagnostic Tools

To ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment, we use the latest diagnostic tools in cardiology, including:

  • **Electrocardiogram (ECG)** – A non-invasive test to monitor heart rhythm and detect abnormal heart patterns.

  • **Echocardiogram** – An ultrasound of the heart to visualize heart valves, chambers, and blood flow.

  • **Stress Testing** – A test to evaluate the heart’s performance under physical stress, commonly used to detect coronary artery disease.

  • **Cardiac Catheterization (Angiogram)** – A procedure to evaluate the blood vessels of the heart for blockages or abnormalities.

  • **24-Hour Holter Monitoring** – Continuous heart monitoring to detect arrhythmias or other irregularities in the heart's electrical activity.

Specialized Programs in Cardiology

We offer specialized cardiac programs to meet the unique needs of individuals with specific heart conditions. These include:

  • **Cardiac Rehabilitation Program** – A structured program to help individuals recover from heart surgery, heart attacks, and other cardiovascular issues through exercise, education, and counseling.

  • **Hypertension Management** – Comprehensive management of high blood pressure through lifestyle modifications, medication, and ongoing monitoring.

  • **Cholesterol Management Program** – A program to help manage cholesterol levels through diet, medication, and lifestyle changes.

  • **Preventive Cardiology** – A focus on preventing heart disease through regular screenings, lifestyle counseling, and early intervention.
